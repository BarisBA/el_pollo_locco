What´s the main goal?
You have to kill the Endboss by throwing bottles at him.
Those bottles can be collected with just colliding with them.
Who else are my enemies?
The only friends you got are the coins and bottles which can be collected by colliding with them.
Everyone else is your enemy. You can kill the chickens by jumping on them.
What´s up with the coins?
If you collect enough coins you can fill your lifebar by pressing G.
Use that before your life run´s out.
